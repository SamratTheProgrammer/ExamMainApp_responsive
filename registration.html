What's New
Read all the information and instructions given in the Centralized Employment Notices (CENs) carefully before filling the registration form to prevent mistakes.
Candidates are allowed to apply only one application for a particular CEN.
Medium of Examination: Questions for CBT will be available in 13 regional Indian languages (viz., Assamese, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Konkani, Malayalam, Manipuri, Marathi, Odia, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu and Urdu) besides Hindi and English.
Fill up all details sought in the online application form before submitting, please re-check and confirm that all the information furnished by you is correct in the Preview Form before final submission.
Applications received without the prescribed fee shall not be considered and summarily rejected. No representation against such rejection will be entertained.
